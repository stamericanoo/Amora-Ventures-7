Sulfuric Acid Plant – 1000 TPD Industrial Design

Project Overview

This project presents a complete industrial design simulation of a 1000 tons/day Sulfuric Acid plant using the Contact Process. The design includes detailed chemical reactions, operating conditions, equipment cost estimation, economic feasibility, and sensitivity analysis.

Plant Capacity

1000 Ton/Day

Annual Production

330,000 Ton/Year

Total Capital Investment

Rp 1.127 T

Detailed Process Description

1. Sulfur Burning Furnace (98%)

S + O₂ → SO₂ (Exothermic)

Operating Temperature: 900 – 1100°C

Pressure: ~1 atm

Main purpose: generate continuous SO₂ gas stream.

2. Gas Cleaning & Drying

Removes dust, moisture, impurities.

Temperature after cooling: 40–80°C

3. Catalytic Converter

2SO₂ + O₂ ⇌ 2SO₃

Catalyst: V₂O₅

Temperature: 420–450°C

4. Absorption Tower

SO₃ + H₂SO₄ → H₂S₂O₇ (Oleum)

5. Dilution / Hydration

H₂S₂O₇ + H₂O → 2H₂SO₄
